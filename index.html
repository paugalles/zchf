Zombie Chef Revamped

A game for 2-4 players

About the game

  As a zombie chef in Zombie Chef Revamped, aim to cook delicious recipes that match customer preferences best and collect the most fame tokens to be crowned the ultimate ghoulish gourmet. This game is designed for 2 to 4 players.

Game Components

  The game includes a Chef card for each player (up to 4), Ingredients deck of 50 cards featuring 5 ingredients: brain, blood, eye, finger, heart, Recipes deck of 16 cards, Customers deck of 16 cards showing customers' preferences and much more.

Gameplay

  The gameplay is quite interesting with a Hunting of Humans and Cooking for Customers phase. In each round, players will get to indulge in a playful bet and then strategically cook for their customers to match their preferences and gain fame tokens.

End of Game

  The game concludes when all customer cards have been exposed. The player with the most fame tokens wins. Are you ready to cook some ghoulish gourmet and beat your fellow chefs at their own game?
