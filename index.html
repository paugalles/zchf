Zombie Chef

Coming Soon

Hunt humans, collect ingredients and cook recipes to satisfy customers and earn fame points.

Components

  • Ingredients card deck
  • Recipes card deck
  • Customers card deck
  • Boost cards deck
  • Two 6-sided dice: one with ingredients symbols, the other with numbers
  • Fame token chips

Gameplay

1. Hunting of Humans

In this phase players use dice and cards for betting and earning ingredient cards.

2. Cooking for Customers

In this phase, players use their ingredient cards to cook recipes.
