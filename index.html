Zombie Chef

Coming Soon

As a zombie chef in Zombie Chef Revamped, aim to cook delicious recipes that match customer preferences best and collect the most fame tokens to be crowned the ultimate ghoulish gourmet. Made for 2 to 4 players

Components

  • Ingredients card deck
  • Recipes card deck
  • Customers card deck
  • Boost cards deck
  • Two 6-sided dice: one with ingredients symbols, the other with numbers
  • Fame token chips

Gameplay

1. Hunting of Humans

In this phase, players use dice to collect ingredient cards. You roll the dice, then try to get an ingredient card that matches the dice result. The player who gets the card closest to the dice result gets a special card for the game.

2. Cooking for Customers

In this phase, players use their ingredient cards to cook recipes. The goal is to match customer preferences with the recipes you cook. Each time a player successfully cooks a recipe, they collect fame tokens. The player who collects the most fame tokens wins the game.
